# Transit & Fleet Facilities: SE Wisconsin Market Intelligence
**Date:** 12/25/25 | **Revision:** 0
**Segment Value:** HIGH - Public agency, 25% TBE goals, recurring maintenance, multi-facility

---

## Executive Summary

Milwaukee County Transit System (MCTS) and related fleet facilities represent a **$500K+ annual painting opportunity** with significant certification advantages:

- **369-bus fleet** requiring facility maintenance
- **3 major operating facilities** (garages, maintenance buildings)
- **25% TBE participation goals** (Milwaukee County)
- **Recent capital investment** ($300M+ modernization)
- **Electric bus transition** driving facility upgrades
- **Recurring maintenance** cycles

**Key Advantage:** Milwaukee County's TBE (Targeted Business Enterprise) goals recognize DBE certification, providing 25% participation requirements on county contracts.

### Milwaukee County Procurement

**Website:** county.milwaukee.gov/EN/Administrative-Services/Procurement

**Bid Posting:** county.milwaukee.gov/EN/Administrative-Services/Procurement/Bids-and-RFPs

## Competitive Landscape

### Who Paints Transit Facilities?

Transit facility painting typically done by:
- Industrial painting contractors
- General commercial painters (office areas)
- Specialty coatings contractors (floors)
- In-house maintenance (touch-up)

## Appendix: Electric Bus Transition

### Impact on Facilities

MCTS is transitioning to electric buses, which requires:

| Upgrade | Painting Impact |
|---------|-----------------|
| Charging infrastructure | Equipment enclosures |
| Electrical systems | Panel/conduit painting |
| Facility modifications | New construction painting |
| Clean environment | Higher finish standards |

### Future Opportunity

Electric bus facilities require:
- Cleaner environments (less diesel residue)
- Higher-quality finishes
- Charging station areas
- Battery storage/maintenance spaces

**Position Now:** As MCTS expands electric fleet, facility upgrades will follow. Build relationship now for future project participation.
