# Chapter 1: Strategy

**Five-year growth strategy and market positioning for LaBrant & Associates.**

## Executive Summary

This chapter outlines LaBrant's strategic roadmap for diversifying beyond multifamily construction while maintaining open-shop operations. The goal: reduce dependence on a small number of GC relationships by expanding into municipal, institutional, and direct-owner markets where certifications provide competitive advantage.
