## Facility Maintenance: Scope of Work Examples

### Handrail Painting (Common Specification)

**Preparation:**
- Clean surfaces of dirt, grease, and loose paint
- Sand or wire brush to remove rust and create profile
- Prime bare metal with rust-inhibitive primer

**Application:**
- 2 coats high-performance exterior enamel
- 2.5-3.5 mils DFT per coat
- Colors to match existing or owner specification

**Typical Pricing Factors:**
- Linear footage of railing
- Height and accessibility
- Condition (light touch-up vs. full repaint)
- Containment requirements

### Structural Steel Touch-Up

**Preparation:**
- SSPC-SP2 or SP3 surface preparation
- Spot prime bare metal
- Feather edges to sound paint

**Application:**
- Match existing coating system
- Industrial enamel or polyurethane topcoat
- Document coating system for owner records
