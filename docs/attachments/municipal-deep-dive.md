## Certification Advantages and Requirements

### City of Milwaukee SBE Program

**Bid Preference:** 10% (up to $30,000 cap) for certified Local Business Enterprises with SBE status

**Participation Goal:** 25% SBE on contracts over $25,000

**SRC Affidavit:** 5% preference (up to $25,000) for Socially-Responsible Contractors

**Compliance Tracking:** B2Gnow system for contracts over $25,000

**Certification Contact:** Office of Equity and Inclusion, (414) 286-5553

### Milwaukee County TBE Program

**Construction Goal:** 25% Targeted Business Enterprise participation

**Professional Services Goal:** 17% TBE participation

**Accepted Certifications:**
- WisUCP DBE (WisDOT, Milwaukee County, City of Madison, Dane County)
- Wisconsin DOA MBE/WBE
- Milwaukee County SBE

**Contact:** CBDP Office, (414) 278-4747

### WisDOT DBE Program - Critical Update

**October 2025 Status:** U.S. DOT changed DBE/ACDBE rules requiring recertification of all 1,296 certified firms. WisDOT has **temporarily paused setting DBE goals** for federally funded contracts until recertification completes.

**Impact:** DBE certification remains valid for Milwaukee County TBE recognition (non-federal)

**Contact:** osecdbe@dot.wi.gov for recertification status

### Wisconsin DHS Lead Certification

**Requirement:** Mandatory for pre-1978 building work disturbing more than 6 SF interior or 20 SF exterior painted surfaces

**Certification Fees:**
- Lead-Safe Renovator: $75-150 (2-4 year validity)
- Abatement Supervisor: $275+

**Competitive Advantage:** Many painting contractors lack this certification, limiting competition on pre-1978 building work (majority of Milwaukee building stock)

**Contact:** DHSAsbestosLead@dhs.wisconsin.gov or (608) 261-6876
