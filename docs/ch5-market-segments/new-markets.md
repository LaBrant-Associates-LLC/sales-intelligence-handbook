## Major Retail: Scheels at Mayfair Mall

Scheels is renovating the 210,000 SF former Boston Store at Mayfair Mall in Wauwatosa. Construction starts 2026, opening Spring 2027. This is the largest retail construction project in the Milwaukee metro.

**Project Details:**
- Location: Mayfair Mall, Wauwatosa (former Boston Store)
- Size: 210,000 SF (nearly double Scheels' Appleton/Eau Claire stores)
- Value: Full renovation of vacant space, estimated $30-50M construction
- Owner: Brookfield Properties (mall owner), Scheels (tenant)
- Timeline: Design Review Board approved late 2025, construction 2026, open Spring 2027
- Estimated Painting Scope: $400K-$800K (retail destination with 84 specialty shops, cafe, arcade, candy shop)

**Contact:**
- City of Wauwatosa Development: Mark Hammond, Development Director
- Brookfield Properties: Jonathan Kramer, President Retail Business

**Action:** Monitor Daily Reporter for GC announcement. Scheels typically uses regional GCs on their stores. Contact Wauwatosa building department for permit applicant info when filed.

## Untapped Market Segments

### Self-Storage Facilities

Growing segment with regular construction. Climate-controlled facilities need interior painting.

**Recent Milwaukee Activity:**
- Wauwatosa Self Storage: 90,284 SF conversion at 5151 W. State St., 444 units
  - Developer: Midland Commercial Development Corp (Steve Rolfe), Luft Consulting (William Wirth), Nelson Development (Jordan Nelson)
  - Architect: Thrive Architects
  - Opening: Summer 2025
- One Stop Self Storage: 3742 W Wisconsin Ave (opened)

**Typical Scope:** Hallways, unit doors, trim, office areas. $25K-$75K per facility.

### Automotive Dealerships

Manufacturers require brand compliance upgrades every few years. Service expansions and EV infrastructure driving renovation activity.

**Major SE Wisconsin Dealer Groups:**
- Boucher Automotive Group: Multiple brands across SE Wisconsin
- Russ Darrow Group: Multiple locations
- Schlossmann Auto Group: Subaru, Honda
- Miller Motor Sales (Burlington): Ford, Chrysler, Dodge, Jeep, Ram

**Approach:** Contact dealer principals or facilities managers directly. Dealerships often bypass traditional GC bidding.

**Typical Scope:** Service area painting, showroom refresh, exterior. $15K-$100K per location.

### Property Management Refresh Contracts

Annual turnover painting creates recurring revenue. Heavy May-August for student housing.

**Target Companies:**
- Bieck Management: Large rental portfolio
- Welcome Home Milwaukee: Scattered-site investment properties
- Trike Property Management: Large multi-unit buildings
- PMI of Greater Milwaukee: National brand, local presence
- Performance Asset Management: SE Wisconsin focus

**Approach:** Offer annual contracts with fixed unit pricing.

### Light Industrial / Warehouse

I-94 corridor seeing significant development. Target owner-occupied facilities where owners select contractors directly.

**Recent Development:**
- HSA Commercial: 1.9M SF across four warehouses (Kenosha, Bristol)
- Park 94 (Mt. Pleasant): 184-acre Class A industrial park

**Target:** 50,000-200,000 SF owner-occupied facilities, not institutional spec buildings.
