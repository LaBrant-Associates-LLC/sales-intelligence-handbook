### Industrial Coating Opportunities

Wastewater treatment facilities require specialized industrial coatings:
- **Tank lining and coating** (digesters, clarifiers, storage tanks)
- **Structural steel protection** (corrosive environments)
- **Pipe coating and lining**
- **Concrete protection** (chemical resistance)
- **Handrail and safety marking**
- **Equipment painting**

### Procurement

MMSD maintains a **vendor registration system** and posts opportunities on DemandStar.

**Registration:** mmsd.diversitycompliance.com
**Opportunities:** demandstar.com (search MMSD)
**Website:** mmsd.com/procurement

### Considerations

MMSD work may require:
- Industrial coating certifications (SSPC, NACE)
- Confined space entry capability
- Safety certifications for treatment plant environments
- Experience with corrosion-resistant coating systems

**Assessment:** Evaluate whether your current capabilities and certifications align with MMSD's industrial requirements. This may be a longer-term opportunity requiring capability development.

## Property Management Relationship Strategy

### Approach for Large Building Owners

Unlike municipal bidding, private property maintenance typically works through:

1. **Vendor Registration**
   - Contact property management company
   - Complete vendor application
   - Provide insurance certificates, references
   - Get on approved vendor list

2. **Relationship Building**
   - Meet with building engineers/facilities managers
   - Understand their maintenance cycles and pain points
   - Demonstrate responsiveness and quality

3. **Competitive Positioning**
   - Emphasize certifications (lead cert for older buildings)
   - Highlight quick response capability
   - Offer competitive T&M rates
   - Provide references from similar facilities

4. **Project Tracking**
   - Monitor tenant improvement projects
   - Build GC relationships for TI subcontracting
   - Watch for major renovation announcements

## Milwaukee Public Schools (MPS)

### Caution: Union-Dominated Segment

MPS construction typically uses union-signatory GCs (JP Cullen, Miron, etc.) per project instructions. **Generally avoid pursuing.**

However, **maintenance painting** may operate differently than new construction:
- Facilities & Maintenance Services handles routine work
- Contract labor RFPs do occur
- Online planroom: mps.aegraphics.com

### If Pursuing

- Monitor: Public Purchase (publicpurchase.com) for MPS bids
- Contact: MPS Facilities & Maintenance Services
- Note: HUB (Historically Underutilized Business) preferences apply
