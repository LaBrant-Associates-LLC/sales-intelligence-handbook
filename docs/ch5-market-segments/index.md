# Chapter 5: Untapped Markets

**Religious Institutions, Self-Storage, Light Industrial, and Emerging Segments**

---

## Executive Summary

Fragmented, relationship-driven markets with low union penetration. These are not experimental--they represent underserved segments where certification advantages and lead-safe capabilities create structural barriers to competition.

**12-Month Pipeline: $800K-$1.6M**

---

## Religious Institutions

### Market Size

- **642+ congregations** in SE Wisconsin
- Lead certification required for pre-1978 buildings
- Low competition from certified contractors
- Typically merit-shop friendly, relationship-driven
