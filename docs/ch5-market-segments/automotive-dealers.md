# Automotive Dealers
**Date:** 12/25/25 | **Revision:** 0
**Segment Value:** HIGH - 100+ dealerships, brand-mandated renovations, consolidated ownership groups

---

## Executive Summary

Automotive dealerships represent a **$1-2M+ annual painting opportunity** in SE Wisconsin with unique characteristics:

- **100+ dealerships** across Milwaukee metro area
- **Manufacturer-mandated renovations** every 10-15 years (IMAGE PROGRAMS)
- **Consolidated ownership** = single decision-maker for multiple facilities
- **Body shop operations** = ongoing industrial painting relationships
- **Non-union environment** in most cases
- **Quality-focused** (brand image critical)

**Key Insight:** Target ownership GROUPS (Russ Darrow, Umansky, Van Horn, etc.) rather than individual dealerships for maximum efficiency.

## Market Dynamics

### Why Dealerships Renovate

| Driver | Frequency | Investment |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| **Manufacturer Image Programs** | Every 10-15 years | $500K-$5M+ |
| **Brand Standard Updates** | Every 5-7 years | $100K-$500K |
| **Ownership Changes** | As needed | Variable |
| **Service Expansion** | Ongoing | $200K-$1M |
| **Showroom Refresh** | Every 7-10 years | $50K-$200K |

### Manufacturer Image Programs

**What Are Image Programs?**

Automotive manufacturers require dealers to maintain specific facility standards. Periodically, they update these standards, triggering mandatory renovations:

| Brand | Current Program | Typical Investment |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Toyota** | Destination Dealer | $1M-$3M |
| **Honda** | Excite Program | $500K-$2M |
| **Nissan** | NREDI 2.0 | $500K-$2M |
| **BMW** | Future Retail | $2M-$5M+ |
| **Mercedes** | MAR 2020 | $2M-$5M+ |
| **Chrysler/Dodge/Jeep/Ram** | Facility Standards | $500K-$2M |
| **Kia** | Design Identity | $500K-$1.5M |
| **Mazda** | Retail Evolution | $300K-$1M |

**Painting Scope in Image Programs:**
- Exterior façade painting
- Interior showroom walls/ceilings
- Service drive-through areas
- Customer lounge spaces
- Office areas
- Signage and branding

## Body Shop Opportunities

### What Are Dealership Body Shops?

Dealer collision centers perform:
- Insurance repairs
- Paint and bodywork
- PDR (paintless dent repair)
- Detailing

**Painting Needs:**
- Booth maintenance (walls, floors, filtration areas)
- Non-production areas (offices, customer waiting)
- Exterior maintenance
- Safety striping and markings

## Competitive Landscape

### Current Competitors

Dealership painting is typically done by:
- Large commercial painters (major renovations)
- Small local contractors (maintenance)
- National facility services (chain accounts)
- In-house maintenance (touch-up only)

### Differentiation

| Factor | Advantage |
|--------|-----------|
| **Local presence** | Faster response than nationals |
| **Commercial capability** | More professional than small contractors |
| **Automotive focus** | Understanding of brand standards |
| **Flexible scheduling** | After-hours capability |
| **Multi-location efficiency** | Group pricing |
