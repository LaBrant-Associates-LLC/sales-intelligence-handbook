## Executive Summary

Direct relationships with large property owners bypass GC markup and create recurring maintenance opportunities. Unlike municipal bidding, private property maintenance works through vendor registration, property management outreach, and relationship building.

## Property Management Relationship Strategy

### Approach for Large Building Owners

1. **Vendor Registration**
   - Contact property management company
   - Complete vendor application
   - Provide insurance, references
   - Get on approved vendor list

2. **Relationship Building**
   - Meet with building engineers/facilities managers
   - Understand maintenance cycles
   - Demonstrate responsiveness and quality

3. **Competitive Positioning**
   - Lead with certifications
   - Highlight quick response capability
   - Offer competitive T&M rates
   - Provide similar facility references

4. **Project Tracking**
   - Monitor tenant improvement projects
   - Build GC relationships for TI subcontracting
